\subsection{Shoelace Formula}
Let $((x_1, y_1), \dots, (x_n, y_n))$ be a not-selfintersecting polygon. Then
$$A=\frac{1}{2}\left | \sum_{i=1}^{n-1}x_iy_{i+1}+x_ny_1-\sum_{i=1}^{n-1}x_{i+i}y_i-x_1y_n\right |$$
